A growing app built with effort, care, and future ambition

Tiger Spark is created by Arctic dev, an independent beginner developer who is actively working to improve the quality, usefulness, and overall experience of the application. The project is built with real effort and a genuine intention to deliver value over time.

Current App Position

Tiger Spark currently does not include paid features or subscriptions. The present focus is on building a stable foundation, improving the product, and creating an experience that feels useful and trustworthy to users.

The app is being developed with long-term thinking. Any future monetization will be introduced carefully and transparently so that users clearly understand what is free, what is optional, and what value is being added.

Developer Message

I am a beginner developer and I work hard on this app. My goal is not only to publish an application, but to keep improving it and make it more valuable with every update.

Future Monetization

In the future, paid options or subscriptions may be added to support development and create sustainable income. These changes are planned as a natural next step once the product grows and delivers stronger value.

Core Principles Behind Tiger Spark

Transparency: open information about the developer, support, and privacy commitments.
Effort: the app is being built with serious attention, learning, and continuous improvement.
Value: the intention is to create something useful, meaningful, and worth returning to.
Fair Growth: monetization, when introduced, is planned to be clear, honest, and respectful to users.

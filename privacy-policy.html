Privacy Policy for Tiger Spark

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Tiger Spark, published by Arctic dev. It is provided to clearly explain how user information is handled.

Simple Summary:

We do not collect personal data, we do not store user data, and we do not share user data with third parties.

Information Collection

Tiger Spark does not collect personal information, device information, location data, contact data, payment data, or any other identifiable user information.

Data Storage

Tiger Spark does not store user data on external servers, private databases, or cloud services. No personal records are maintained by the developer.

Data Sharing

No user data is sold, transferred, shared, or disclosed to third parties because no user data is collected in the first place.

Children's Privacy

Since Tiger Spark does not collect any personal data, it does not knowingly process information relating to children or any other users.

Policy Updates

If the app changes in the future and begins to use features that require data processing, this Privacy Policy will be updated before such changes are applied. Any future update will be written clearly and published publicly.
